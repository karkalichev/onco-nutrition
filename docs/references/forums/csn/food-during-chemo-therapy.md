# What food to eat while on chemo therapy

**Original question:** I wonder if there's any limitations on what food to eat while on chemo therapy. Some say avoid red meat, eat vegetables rich in protein since our body needs energy while we're under treatment.

## Key peer responses

- **Keep sugars down** when possible; don't over-restrict if losing weight
- **Protein is very important** — red meat gave one member energy boost
- **Avoid high antioxidants** — counteract chemo (green tea, blueberries, pomegranate)
- **Eat whatever you can tolerate** — chemo changes taste completely
- **"White foods" phase:** mashed potatoes, plain rice, angel hair pasta, vanilla pudding, cream of wheat — then return to healthy eating on good days
- **Food diary** to track tolerance; side effects change with each regimen
- **Oncologist restrictions vary:** FOLFOX → avoid green tea, turmeric; irinotecan/Erbitux → avoid St. John's wort, grapefruit
- **Cookbook recommended:** *Eating Well Through Cancer* (Holly Clegg & Gerald Miletello, M.D.)
- **Best advice from thread:** "Just live" — enjoy appetite when you have it
