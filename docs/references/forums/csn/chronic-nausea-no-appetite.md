# chronic nausea, no appetite

**Context:** Caregiver asking about father with lung cancer mets (brain, spinal cord). Completed radiation 11/2007. No appetite, chronic nausea/vomiting after eating, nutrients via GI tube. Vomits undigested food 2–3 days later.

## Peer response

- Small cell lung cancer survivor (7 years): forced self to eat **homemade milkshake**, **toast with margarine**, **cups of soup (chicken type)** despite continuous vomiting
- "I know this is a terrible disease but is how I got thru treatments"

## Relevance for assistant
- Extreme cases need **medical team**, not diet tips alone
- Soft/calorie-dense liquids when solids fail: milkshakes, soup, toast
- Caregiver-facing queries are common ("my father won't eat")
