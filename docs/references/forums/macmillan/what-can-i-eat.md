# The Online Community asks "What can I eat?"

Macmillan community blog (Jun 2023) summarizing food/diet discussions across forums.

## Key points (evidence-aligned)
- **No single diet** suits all cancer patients — depends on weight status and side effects
- **No diet has enough evidence** to "beat cancer" on its own
- Seek **registered dietitian** with oncology experience — many "nutrition experts" lack qualifications
- Head & neck cancer forum especially active on food challenges

## Patient comments from blog
- Oncologist: **"eat whatever you fancy"** — but patient has diabetes, comfort eating, fluid retention concerns
- Caregiver: cut refined sugar, processed food, dairy; **lack of appetite** despite small meals, snacks, smoothies; waiting for "green powder" supplement

## Patient language patterns
- "what can I eat"
- "lack of appetite is a big problem"
- "get my immune system as strong as possible"
- "food is a big problem"
