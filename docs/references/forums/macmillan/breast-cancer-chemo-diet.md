# Breast cancer chemo and diet

**Original question:** Recently had lumpectomy + lymph nodes. Starting chemo and radiotherapy soon. Has anyone changed their diet? Want to get immune system as strong as possible.

## Peer responses

- **Normal healthy diet** with fruit and veg; no major changes if already eating well
- **Smaller portions** during treatment
- **Stay hydrated** — squash, water
- **Nausea snacks:** ginger biscuits, ice cream, ice lollies; snacking helped queasiness
- **Pre-plan meals** for post-chemo dip days — comfort food: shepherd's pie, bolognese; partner adds extra veg
- **When nauseous:** toast and porridge
- **When taste is horrible:** pineapple and cucumber most bearable
- **Eat for texture** when taste is gone — brain remembers liked foods
- **Avoid grapefruit** — interacts with chemo drugs (nurse advice)
- **Lots of protein** for energy, tissue healing, immunity; maintain weight during treatment
- **Little and often** after treatment when feeling sick
- **Maggie's Centres** run food workshops for people with cancer

## Patient language patterns
- "immune system as strong as possible"
- "won't know how it hits me until I'm in it"
- "everything tasted horrible"
- "eat for texture"
- "little and often"
