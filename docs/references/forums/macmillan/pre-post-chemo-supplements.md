# Pre & Post Chemo — supplement/diet suggestions

**Original question:** Starting chemo soon — 3× EC + 3× docetaxel. Any supplements, diet or anything to stay fit and healthy?

## Peer responses

1. **Vitamin D** — if low, team should have flagged it; take if advised
2. **Probiotic for gut health** — microbes take a pounding; prunes/dried apricots for steroid constipation
3. **⚠️ Don't take anything without checking team** — supplements interact with chemo
4. **Probiotics/live foods:** one oncologist said **avoid until 1 month after chemo** — conflicting advice in same thread
5. **Basics:** 8h sleep, healthy unprocessed food, exercise, take it easy, ask for help

## Assistant guidance implication
- Always route supplement questions to **"ask your oncologist"**
- Present **conflicting peer advice** carefully — don't pick a side without medical context

## Patient language patterns
- "supplements before during and after chemo"
- "keep me as fit and healthy as practicable"
- "vit D tablets"
