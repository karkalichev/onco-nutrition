# Communities and forums: oncology patients

Catalog of places where patients share experience about food, symptoms, and treatment.  
**Updated:** 2026-05-21

> Peer advice ≠ medical advice. Many posts contain contradictory or unverified information (keto, antioxidants, “superfoods”). For RAG, use for **language and real questions**, not as the sole source of truth.

> **Archived thread files** under `forums/` remain in **original language** (mostly English). Bulgarian **web** archives under `web/` are kept in Bulgarian for retrieval.
